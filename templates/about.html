About Me

This is what I do.

I am a student of Applied Computer Science at the Warsaw University of Technology, with a strong passion for creating innovative solutions and automating existing processes. My main interests lie in backend development and artificial intelligence, where I enjoy building smart, efficient systems that solve real-world problems.

In addition to my academic and programming pursuits, I’m deeply interested in both tennis and the automotive world. I find that sports keep me focused and disciplined, while my enthusiasm for cars fuels my curiosity and attention to detail.

Whether I’m developing applications, exploring the possibilities of AI, or just enjoying a good match on the tennis court, I’m always eager to learn and push boundaries. This blog is a space where I share not only my technical journey but also insights, ideas, and the things that inspire me along the way.
